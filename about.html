The Luxury Jewels Story

A legacy of craftsmanship passed down through generations

Our Heritage

Founded in 1985 by master jeweler Rajesh Kumar, Luxury Jewels began as a small atelier in the heart of Delhi's historic jewelry district. What started as a modest family business has grown into one of India's most respected luxury jewelry houses.

Our Craftsmanship

Every piece we create is handcrafted by our team of skilled artisans who combine traditional techniques with modern innovation. Our commitment to perfection means that only the finest ethically-sourced materials make it into our collections.

Our Promise

We believe that luxury jewelry should be more than beautiful – it should tell a story, create a connection, and stand the test of time. With every creation, we promise uncompromising quality, exceptional design, and personalized service.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Luxury Jewels into what it is today

1985

The Beginning

Luxury Jewels was founded in Delhi's historic Chandni Chowk by Rajesh Kumar, a third-generation jeweler with a vision to blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.

1995

Expansion

After a decade of growth, we opened our flagship store in South Delhi, introducing our creations to a wider audience and establishing partnerships with international suppliers of premium gemstones.

2005

Innovation

We pioneered the use of advanced 3D modeling and CAD technology in our design process, allowing for greater precision and the ability to create increasingly complex and innovative pieces.

2010

Global Recognition

Our signature "Lotus Collection" was featured in Vogue India and earned international acclaim, leading to collaborations with renowned fashion designers and celebrities.

2020

Digital Transformation

We embraced e-commerce and virtual consultations, making our exclusive collections accessible to clients worldwide while maintaining our commitment to personalized service.

Today

Looking Forward

While honoring our heritage, we continue to evolve and innovate, focusing on sustainable practices and creating jewelry that will be treasured for generations to come.

Meet Our Master Artisans

With over 70 years of combined experience, our artisans bring unparalleled expertise to every piece of jewelry we create.

Elena Ivanova

Master Goldsmith

Elena Ivanova

With 25 years of experience, Elena specializes in traditional goldsmithing techniques passed down through generations. Her meticulous attention to detail has earned her international recognition.

Trained in St. Petersburg, she brings classic European craftsmanship to every piece she creates, specializing in intricate filigree work and complex metal forming.

Marcus Chen

Gem Specialist

Marcus Chen

With 18 years in gemology, Marcus has developed an expert eye for selecting the finest gemstones. He travels globally to source rare and exceptional stones for our premium collections.

Certified by the Gemological Institute of America, his expertise in diamond grading and colored gemstone identification ensures that only the highest quality gems make it into our pieces.

Sofia Rodriguez

Design Director

Sofia Rodriguez

A graduate of the Paris School of Jewelry Arts, Sofia brings contemporary vision while honoring timeless design principles. Her award-winning designs have been featured in luxury publications worldwide.

With a background in fine art and architecture, Sofia creates pieces that balance aesthetic beauty with wearable comfort, resulting in jewelry that becomes an extension of the wearer.
