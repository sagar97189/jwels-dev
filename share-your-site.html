Share Your Jewelry Website

Choose one of the following options to share your beautiful jewelry website with friends and family.

Option 1: Share on Your Local Network (Easiest)

This option works when you and your friends are on the same WiFi network.

  1. Double-click the start-server.bat file
  2. Look for your IP address in the window that appears
  3. Share the URL (something like http://192.168.1.100:8080) with your friends
  4. They can type this address in their browser to see your site
Start Local Server

Option 2: Share Publicly (Advanced)

This option lets you share with anyone on the internet, no matter where they are.

Note: This requires installing ngrok, a free tool for creating public URLs.
  1. If you don't have ngrok, download it here
  2. Double-click the share-public.bat file
  3. Follow the instructions in the window that appears
  4. You'll get a public URL to share with anyone
Share Publicly
